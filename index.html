Corporate Profile

The Gold Standard in
Industrial Fabrication.

Since its inception in 2012, M/s Shree Engineering Works has specialized in high-performance auto-body work and heavy industrial fabrication. Headquartered in the industrial hub of Shirwal, Satara & Mumbai we have built a reputation for delivering precision-engineered solutions for India’s top automotive manufacturers.

Our facility spans 8,000 sq. ft. and is equipped with advanced manufacturing technology. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we operate with a "Zero-Defect" philosophy, ensuring every project meets rigorous international safety and durability standards.
